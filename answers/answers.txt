# What are all the primary types and how many pokemon have that type?
SELECT pokemon.types.name, COUNT(*) FROM pokemon.pokemons INNER JOIN pokemon.types ON pokemon.pokemons.primary_type = pokemon.types.id GROUP BY pokemon.types.name;

# How many pokemon only belong to one trainer and no other?
